All about Yu.

Yu is all about minimal, simple aesthetic garments. We have made every
piece of our cloth with the most care that makes you feel Yu.

For him, for her,

for everyone.

Yu collective started with the idea of creating simplicity & identity in fashion.
From sourcing to stitching, to the creatives, everything was crafted to portray
the vision of fashion in simple terms. Be it the colors or the statements made
we felt that less could resonate more.
